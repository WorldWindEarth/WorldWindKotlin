Triangle Mesh Picking Tutorial

A tap or pointer hover first resolves the visible mesh by its pick color ID. The WorldWindow then renders just that mesh in a depth-encoding pass, samples the clicked depth value, and unprojects the screen point back into 3D space.

The tutorial uses that 3D point to highlight the selected mesh, place a small sphere marker exactly on the picked location, draw a radial line from the globe center to 3000 km above the surface through the hit point, and show a fixed overlay with the picked object plus the geographic and Cartesian coordinates. The line turns red for mesh hits, green for earth hits, and disappears when nothing is hit.
