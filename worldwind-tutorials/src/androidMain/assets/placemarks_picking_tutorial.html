Placemarks Picking Tutorial

Demonstrates how to pick and highlight Placemarks.

Tapping a placemark will toggle its highlighted state.

Example

PlacemarksPickingFragment.kt

The PlacemarksPickingFragment class extends the BasicGlobeFragment and overrides the createWorldWindow method. Here we create and add a RenderableLayer with four Placemarks to the globe. We also override the default WorldWindowController with a PickNavigateController inner class to respond to touch events on the Placemarks.
